Surf Brand Redesign


Brand Identity | Visual Communication Design

BRAND IDENTITY

Surf Incubator is a company that helps technology startups grow and develop by providing an affordable and professional environment. I identified Surf’s brand attributes as technological and credible, which came from its target (technology startups) and its missions (being supportive).

WEB REDESIGN

I redesigned the homepage and room reservation page (above the fold). The design went through three iterations.

Iteration 1

Iteration 2

Iteration 3

Home PAGE

The most possible users of a company’s homepage are its potential clients. The goal of visiting the homepage is to get important information about Surf, including what it does, what it looks like, and its service. Based on those information, the user determines whether or not to join Surf. Therefore, I made several changes to make the information delivered more effectively.

I made three major changes to the original homepage:

  1. Change 1: highlighted “Join” button
    I moved the “Join” button to the very right and changed its background color to distinguish it from other links so that it is very easy to notice, which fulfill one of Surf’s mission: having people join it. Its button-liked look creates a feeling that the register process would be simple and fast.

  2. Change 2: categorized contents
    On the original homepage, all the information was listed in bullet points and is mixed with different types of services Surf can provide. Audience may find the information too much and not be willing to read carefully. Therefore, I divided them into several categories and put the texts together with corresponding images to make the information impressive and easy to read.

  3. Change 3: added membership information
    After organizing the bullet points, there was extra space for me to put information. Since the goal was to let visitors know more about the company, I decided to add the membership information, which I found on the other page
