Doki·Doki

My Contribution: Research | Wireframe | Prototype | UI Design | Visual Design

PROBLEM

Approximately 610,000 people die of heart disease in the United States yearly - accounting for 1 in every 4 deaths, yet the common person has little to none knowledge on heart diseases unless their family had heart disease histories.

Our goal is to bring heart disease awareness and prevention to people, as well as to motivate them to take an active role in their health. Because...

hearts shouldn’t stop beating early.

TARGETED USERS

The targeted users are students and working professionals who spend most of their time studying and working and are likely to neglect to live a healthy lifestyle. Based on our findings, we created a persona.

INITIAL RESEARCH

Our initial research showed that there were three problems that could cause heart disease due to unstable blood pressure.

Fortunately, these problems are totally correctable, which were great entry points for us to solve the problem.

USER RESEARCH

We conducted user research to understand lifestyles of our targeted users, their concerns about their health, and current technology they use to track health.

From the survey, we also learned that wearable could be a good option.

DESIGN

We decided to move forward with a wearable, since compared with the heart health monitor on the market, a wearable is portable, provides real-time information, easy to understand, and affordable.

The idea is a wearable paired with smart phone app. The wearable is used mainly for gathering data from user and the app is used for data interpretation, notifications and detailed information.

I also thought of using gradient outer ring on the wearable to indicate general health condition for long term motivation because lifestyle change is a slow process.
