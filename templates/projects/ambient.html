Ambient


Individual Project | Rapid Prototyping | Interaction Design | UI Design

It's a calendar + clock.

PROBLEM

At the beginning of the year, I bought myself a planner. Then I consecutively received 2 desktop calendars and a wall calendar as gifts. Also, I have a calendar widget on my laptop and I sometimes use Apple’s calendar on my iphone, too.

It’s nice to have different forms of calendars because they allow me to access to date and plan quickly almost in any situation, but using them the same time can be inefficient and cause inconsistency.

Therefore, I wanted to design a calendar that is accessible in several situations and also consistent and efficient to use.

USER RESEARCH

Initially I wanted to design for myself, but I wanted to learn about other people’s behaviors of checking calendar through interview, so that I could have a better understanding from different perspectives.

She walks to the calendar on the wall and easily finds the information she needs. After she confirms the date, she updates the event to the calendar.
